# EXPERIMENTS

Each experiment gets its own folder. Every experiment is documented —
before, during, and after. A documented failure is a successful
contribution.

## How to start an experiment

1. Propose it in an [Experiment issue](../../../issues/new?template=experiment.md)
   or a [Discussion](../../../discussions).
2. When it's ready to run, create a folder: `EXPERIMENTS/<short-name>/`
3. Copy [TEMPLATE.md](TEMPLATE.md) into it as `README.md` and fill in
   the Problem, Hypothesis, and Method **before** you start.
4. Update Results and Lessons **as you go**, not just at the end.
5. Promote the key lesson to [DISCOVERIES/](../DISCOVERIES/) when done.

## Rules

- Every experiment states its hypothesis before running. No
  retro-fitting.
- Results are published whatever they are. Negative results are
  results.
- Experiments involving real people require their consent before
  anything about them is published. People come before technology.
