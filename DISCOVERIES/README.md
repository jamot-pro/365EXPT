# DISCOVERIES

Every important learning from the experiment lives here.
**Failures included.** Knowledge is more valuable than perfection.

## What belongs here

- Lessons promoted from completed experiments in [EXPERIMENTS/](../EXPERIMENTS/)
- Interview write-ups (Mission 7 — talking to people affected by AI)
- Research findings on existing solutions
- Documented failures — what we tried, why it didn't work, what we'd do differently
- Patterns the community notices across problems and experiments

## How to add a discovery

1. Create a file: `YYYY-MM-DD-short-title.md`
2. Use this structure:

```markdown
# [Title]

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Contributor(s):** @handle(s)
**Source:** [experiment folder / interview / research / discussion link]

## What we learned

## Evidence

## What this changes

## Open questions
```

3. Open a pull request.
